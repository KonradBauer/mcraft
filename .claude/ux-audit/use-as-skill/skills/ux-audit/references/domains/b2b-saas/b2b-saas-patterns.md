## A. The SaaS Landing Standard
**Use when**: High-velocity B2B Sales, Direct Response.
**Core Narrative**: "Linear persuasion to overcome objections."
**Structure**:
1. **Hero**: Promise + Visual (UI) + Trust (Logos).
2. **Agitation**: The Pain of the Status Quo.
3. **The Zig-Zag**: Feature/Benefit alternating grid.
4. **The Switch Trigger**: Address the pain of switching (Migration, Ease).
5. **Social Proof Bar**: "Trusted by...".
6. **Close**: Risk Reversal (Guarantee, No Credit Card).
