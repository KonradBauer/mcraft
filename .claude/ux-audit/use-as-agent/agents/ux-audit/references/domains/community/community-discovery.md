# Community: Discovery

Domain knowledge for community and membership sites.
Part of: references/domains/community/

---

## Key Assumptions to Form

- **Community model**: Creator-led, peer-led, or brand-led?
- **Membership model**: Free, paid tiers, or hybrid (free + premium)?
- **Community platform**: Circle, Discord, Slack, Mighty Networks, custom-built?
- **Content type**: Discussion-based, event-based, course-integrated, resource library, or mixed?
- **Target community size**: Intimate (< 100), mid-scale (100-1,000), or large-scale (1,000+)?
- **Cold-start status**: Existing community migrating, or building from zero?
- **Moderation model**: Creator-moderated, community-moderated, or professional moderation?
- **Identity specificity**: Broad interest group or tightly defined professional/identity niche?

---

## Common Project Shapes

- **Creator community with paid membership**: Landing page + pricing + about. Creator-Led archetype. Pattern A + B. Primary challenge: proving value behind the gate.
- **Professional network with free access**: Landing page + member directory preview + application. Professional Network archetype. Pattern A + C. Primary challenge: qualifying members without creating friction.
- **Course + community hybrid**: Course sales page drives enrollment; community is a retention and engagement layer. Pattern A adapted with course content integration. Primary challenge: community is secondary to course — must demonstrate standalone value.
- **Brand community**: Company builds community around its product or category. Interest-Based archetype. Pattern A + C. Primary challenge: authenticity — members must feel it's their space, not a marketing channel.

---

## Unique Constraints

- **Cold start problem**: A community needs members to attract members. The landing page must create the perception of activity even when early. Strategies: founder credibility, waitlist framing, charter member positioning, imported content from other channels.
- **Content moderation**: Member-generated content means quality variance. The landing page must show curated highlights, not a random feed. Moderation policy affects community culture and must be established before launch.
- **Platform choice impacts UX**: The community platform (Circle vs Discord vs Slack vs custom) determines what's possible in the member experience. The landing page lives outside the platform — it's a recruitment layer, not the community itself.
- **The ghost town problem**: The #1 community killer. Every design decision must counteract the perception of inactivity. Activity proof components, recent timestamps, and member testimonials are non-negotiable trust signals.

---

## Top Tasks (in priority order)

1. **Evaluate community value** — "What will I get as a member?"
2. **Assess activity level** — "Is this community actually active?"
3. **Understand pricing** — "What does it cost, and is there a free option?"
4. **Sign up / join** — Complete the membership flow with minimal friction.
5. **Find first engagement** — Post-join: "What do I do first?" (onboarding, not landing page).

---

## Primary Conversion

- **Free community**: Member signup (email + profile creation).
- **Paid community**: Membership purchase (tier selection + payment).
- **Hybrid**: Free signup with upgrade path to paid tiers.

In all cases, signup is not success — **activation is**. First meaningful engagement (first post, first event attended, first reply received) is the true conversion metric.

---

## Top Anxieties and Trust Signals

| Anxiety | Trust Signal |
|---------|-------------|
| "Is it active or a ghost town?" | Activity proof banner with recent metrics and timestamps |
| "Will I fit in?" | Member testimonials about belonging, community values, identity-specific language |
| "Is it worth the price?" | Free tier availability, "Cancel anytime," member outcome stories |
| "What platform is it on?" | Platform logo/name + brief explanation of the member experience |
| "Can I actually access the creator?" | Creator engagement proof — AMA schedule, response examples, "Creator posts X times/week" |
| "What if I join and it's not for me?" | Cancellation policy, refund terms, free tier as low-risk entry |
| "Is my data safe / who sees my profile?" | Privacy policy summary, profile visibility controls, moderation policy |
