# Course: Discovery

Domain knowledge for online course and education product sites.
Part of: references/domains/course/

---

## Key Assumptions to Form
- **Course model**: Solo creator or platform?
- **Delivery format**: Cohort-based or self-paced?
- **Price point**: <$50 impulse / $50-500 considered / $500+ premium?
- **Audience origin**: Existing audience (creator already has following?) or cold traffic?
- **Credential included**: Certificate or portfolio piece offered?
- **Free competition level**: How much free content exists on this topic? (high = harder sell)
- **Content format**: Video-only / mixed media / live sessions?
- **Completion support**: Self-directed / community / mentorship?

---

## Common Project Shapes
- **Solo creator course** (Teachable, Podia): Single landing page + checkout. Solo Creator archetype.
- **Cohort-based program** (Maven, Reforge): Application/enrollment page + alumni showcase. Cohort-Based Program archetype.
- **Platform course listing** (Udemy, Coursera): Catalog + individual course pages. Platform Course archetype.
- **Course + community hybrid**: Landing page + community preview + membership pricing. Solo Creator with community layer.

---

## Unique Constraints
- **Intangible product**: Cannot preview before buying — all trust must be established pre-purchase through curriculum transparency, instructor credibility, and outcome proof
- **Free content competition**: Every paid course competes with free YouTube, blog posts, and documentation. The value proposition must justify premium beyond raw information
- **Instructor credibility is make-or-break**: Unlike SaaS (where the product speaks) or e-commerce (where the item is tangible), the instructor's authority is the primary purchase driver
- **Completion rates affect testimonial supply**: Low completion → few outcome stories → harder to sell. Course design directly impacts marketing effectiveness

---

## Top Tasks (Priority Order)
1. Evaluate course content (curriculum review)
2. Assess instructor credibility
3. Read student outcomes / testimonials
4. Compare price vs alternatives
5. Enroll / purchase

---

## Primary Conversion
Course enrollment — either direct purchase or free enrollment (for freemium/platform models). One-time transaction, not subscription. No trial period equivalent.

---

## Top Anxieties and Trust Signals

| Anxiety | Trust Signal |
|---------|-------------|
| "Is this worth it vs free content?" | Outcome metrics from past students, structured path vs scattered content, community/accountability |
| "Will I actually finish?" | Estimated time commitment, flexible pacing, community support, bite-sized modules |
| "Is the instructor qualified?" | Credentials, notable work, student count, media appearances, professional photo |
| "What if it's not good?" | Money-back guarantee (prominent, near CTA), preview lessons if available |
| "Is this up to date?" | Last updated date, version number, recent testimonials, current-year enrollment count |
